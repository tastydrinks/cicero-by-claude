% Ad Atticum 3.11 --- headnote
% ad-atticum-03-11, 28 June 58 BC, written at Thessalonica

\textit{Cicero to Atticus, written from Thessalonica on the
fourth day before the Kalends of July (28 June) 58 BC. A short
letter, holding pattern: still waiting for Atticus's news of
the proceedings at Rome, with the option of moving towards
Atticus open if the rumours of softening prove true. The
sharper note in \textsection 2 is Cicero asking Atticus to
stop consoling him, and not to begin rebuking him --- the
rebuke is the harder to bear because it makes him miss
Atticus's grief, which alone could match his own.}
