% Ad Familiares 13.41 --- headnote
% ad-familiares-13-41, ca. early 58 BC, written at Rome

\textit{Cicero to L. Culleolus, written from Rome around early
58 BC, follow-up to \textit{Fam.} 13.42. Lucceius is now
reporting back through his agents: the proconsul has done what
he was asked, and Pompey gives Culleolus particular thanks
``every time he sees me.'' The closing reference to a previous
letter ``about the commonwealth and these affairs and our
reflections'' is to a piece of correspondence now lost.}
