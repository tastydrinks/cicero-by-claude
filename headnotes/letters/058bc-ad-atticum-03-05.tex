% Ad Atticum 3.5 --- headnote
% ad-atticum-03-05, 10 April 58 BC, written at Thurii

\textit{Cicero to Atticus, written from Thurii on 10 April 58
BC, on the road south. Brief, dazed, the apophthegm at the
centre: \textit{inimici mei mea mihi, non me ipsum ademerunt}
--- ``my enemies have taken from me my own things, not
myself.'' Terentia's thanks (her debts to Atticus through
this period are large and ongoing); Cicero's own being
``unable to write,'' the line that recurs from \textit{Att.}
3.3 onward.}
