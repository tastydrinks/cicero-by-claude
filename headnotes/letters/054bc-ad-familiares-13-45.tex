% Ad Familiares 13.45 --- headnote
% ad-familiares-13-45, ca. 45 BC, Rome (placeholder year_range)

\textit{Cicero to Appuleius, proquaestor (probably of Asia, ca.
46--45 BC, between the proconsulship of Q. Cornificius and that of
Q. Mucius Scaevola). L. Egnatius the Roman knight --- one of
Cicero's banker friends, named again in \textit{Fam.} 13.43 to
Quinctius Gallus and elsewhere --- has business in the province
to be looked after, in the person of his slave Anchialus. The
closing formula is the standard plea of recommendation letters:
\textit{see to it that he comes to know I wrote you diligently
enough.}}
