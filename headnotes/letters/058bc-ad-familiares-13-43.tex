% Ad Familiares 13.43 --- headnote
% ad-familiares-13-43, ca. late 59 BC, written at Rome

\textit{Cicero to Quinctius Gallus, written at Rome around late
59 BC --- the date imprecise; the Perseus dateline notes
``some months before letter 44'' (Fam. 13.44 to Fadius Gallus,
ca. early 58 BC), so the letter sits in the run-up to the
exile. A short recommendation for L. Oppius, who handles in
Asia Minor (the trading town of Philomelium) the business of
L. Egnatius Rufus --- a Roman knight Cicero counts among his
closest connections.}
