% Ad Atticum 3.21 --- headnote
% ad-atticum-03-21, 28 October 58 BC, written at Thessalonica

\textit{Cicero to Atticus, written from Thessalonica on the
fifth day before the Kalends of November (28 October) 58 BC.
A short note in a thirty-day silence. Cicero is still at
Thessalonica, still planning the move to Epirus; the silence
suggests that the consular elections of 57 BC and the
preparations of the new tribunician college are reaching some
fixed point at Rome that requires no longer letters.}
