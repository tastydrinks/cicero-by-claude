\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Each day, or rather as the days run on, I send you a shorter letter; for each day I more suspect that you have already set out for Epirus. Still, just so you know I have looked after your commission --- the one I mentioned earlier --- Pompey says that he will nominate five new prefects each year on the ground of their exemption from jury service. I had spent three days with Pompey and at his house, and on 22 May I was setting out for Brundisium. I left him behind as a most distinguished citizen, and most ready to ward off the dangers we now fear. I shall be looking for a letter from you, both to know what you are doing and to know where you are.
