\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} I will indeed make use of the postponement, and it was very good of you to let me know --- and to do it in such a way that I received your letter at a moment when I was not expecting one, and that you wrote from the games themselves. I have, to be sure, some business to do at Rome; but we shall follow on two days later.
