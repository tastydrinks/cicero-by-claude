\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} The whole of my route is left uncertain by the expectation of your letters dispatched on the Kalends of August. For if there is hope, we shall make for Epirus; if not, we shall follow Cyzicus or some other place. Your own letters --- the more often I read them, the less hope they make in me: when they have been read, the very thing they brought as hope they undo. So that one easily sees you are obliged both to console and to tell the truth. I ask you, plainly: whatever you shall know to be so, write to me as it is; whatever you shall think, so write. Sent the twelfth day before the Kalends [of September].
