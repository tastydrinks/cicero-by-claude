\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Your promptness was more welcome to me than the matter itself. For what could be more outrageous? But by now we have grown hard to such things, and have stripped off all humanity. I was expecting your letter today --- not, of course, to learn anything new from it; for what could there be? But all the same ---
