\ciceroLetterOpener{M. CICERO S. D. M. MARIO.}

\ciceroSection{1} On the 14th day before the Kalends I reached the Cumaean estate with your Libo --- or rather ours. I mean to go on to the Pompeian property at once, but I shall let you know before I do. I always want you to be well, and certainly while I am here, for you see how long it will be before we are together again. So if you have any engagement set with your gout, see to it that you put it off for another day. Take care, then, that you are well, and look for me within the next two or three days.
