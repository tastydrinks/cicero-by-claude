\ciceroLetterOpener{M. CICERO IIII VIRIS ET DECVRIONIBVS S. D.}

\ciceroSection{1} My ties of connection with Q. Hippius are so weighty that nothing could be closer than the bond between us. Were it not so, I would follow my usual practice and trouble you in nothing. You yourselves are my best witnesses that, though I was satisfied there was nothing I could not obtain from you, I have nevertheless never wished to be a burden on you.

\ciceroSection{2} I therefore ask you most earnestly, again and again, that for the sake of my standing you deal as generously as possible with C. Valgius Hippianus, and settle the matter with him so that he may have, free and exempt, the holding which he possesses in the Fregellan country and which he has bought from you. If I obtain this from you, I shall reckon myself to have been treated with the highest kindness.
