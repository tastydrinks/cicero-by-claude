\ciceroLetterOpener{M. CICERO P. SERVILIO CONLEGAE S.}

\ciceroSection{1} Because your good will toward me is no secret, it comes about that many wish to be recommended to you through me; and I, for my part, sometimes dispense my recommendations indiscriminately, but for the most part to those bound to me, as at the present time. For I have the closest friendship and intimacy with T. Ampius Balbus. His freedman, T. Ampius Menander, an honest and modest man, highly thought of both by his patron and by me, I recommend to you in the strongest terms. You will do me a very welcome service if you will oblige him in whatever respects you can without inconvenience to yourself. That you should do so, I most earnestly beg of you, again and again.
