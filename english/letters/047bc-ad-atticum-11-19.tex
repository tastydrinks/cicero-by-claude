\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Since I had the chance to send a letter with your own people, I have not let it pass, although I had nothing to write. You for your part write to me less often than you used to, and more briefly --- because, I suppose, you have nothing you think I could be glad to read or hear. Even so, I should like you to write whatever there is, of whatever kind. There is one thing in particular for me to wish for, if anything can be done about peace; for that I have no hope at all; but because you from time to time hint at it in passing, you force me to hope for what is hardly to be hoped for.

\ciceroSection{2} Philotimus is said to be coming on the Ides of Sextilis. I have nothing further about him. I should like you to write back to me on the points I wrote to you about before. I have time enough only for this --- to be on my guard, amid the worst of circumstances, where I have never in my life been on my guard at all. Farewell. The eleventh day before the Kalends of Sextilis.
