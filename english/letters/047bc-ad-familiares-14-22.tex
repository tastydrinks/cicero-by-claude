\ciceroLetterOpener{TVLLIVS S. D. TERENTIAE SVAE.}

If you are well, it is well; I am well. Day after day we are waiting for our couriers. If they come, perhaps we shall be in a position to know what we have to do, and shall let you know at once. Take careful care of your health. Farewell. The Kalends of September.
