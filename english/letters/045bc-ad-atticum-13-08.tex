\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Plainly there was nothing for me to write to you about; you had only just left, and a little later you had sent back the triple-tablets. Please see to it that the packet is delivered to Vestorius, and put someone on the job of finding out whether Q.~Staberius' estate, or any other, is up for sale in the territory of Pompeii or Nola. Send me, please, Brutus' epitome of the Caelian annals, and from Philoxenus Panaetius \textit{On Providence} \textit{[Greek: Panaitiou peri Pronoias]}. I shall see you on the Ides with your people.
