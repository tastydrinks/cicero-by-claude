\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ACILIO PROCONSVLI S.}

\ciceroSection{1} I have an inherited tie of hospitality with Lyso of Lilybaeum, the son of Lyso, and he shows me marked attention; I have come to know him as a man worthy both of his father and of his grandfather, for the family is one of the highest standing. I therefore commend to you in the strongest terms his fortunes and his household, and earnestly ask of you that you see to it that he understands my recommendation to have served him, with you, both as a help and as a mark of honour.
