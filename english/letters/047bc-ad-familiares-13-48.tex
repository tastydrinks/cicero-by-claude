\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO C. SEXTILIO RVFO QVAESTORI S. D.}

I commend to you all the Cypriots, but the people of Paphos most especially (and whatever favours you do them will be the most welcome to me); and I do it with all the greater pleasure, because I judge it conducive also to your own credit, of which I am the partisan, that since you are the first quaestor to come into that island, you should put in place the practices the rest will follow. These you will achieve the more easily, I trust, if you choose to follow both the regulation of your kinsman Publius Lentulus and what has been laid down by me; and I am confident this will be a thing of great credit to you.
