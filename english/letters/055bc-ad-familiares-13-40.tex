\ciceroLetterOpener{M. CICERO S. D. Q. ANCHARIO Q. F. PROCOS.}

\ciceroSection{1} Lucius and Gaius Aurelius, sons of Lucius — with whom themselves, and with their father, an excellent man, I am on most familiar terms — I commend to you most warmly: young men adorned with all the best accomplishments, my closest friends, most worthy of your friendship. If any commendation of mine has ever availed with you (which I know many have very greatly availed), let this one prevail, I ask. If you should treat them honourably and generously, you will both have attached to yourself most grateful and excellent young men, and you will have done me a most welcome service.
