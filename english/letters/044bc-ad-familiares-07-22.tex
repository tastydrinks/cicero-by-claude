\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO TREBATIO S.}

\ciceroSection{1} You were making fun of me yesterday over the cups, because I had said it was an open question whether an heir could rightly bring an action of theft for a theft committed before the inheritance. So although I came home well in my wine and late, I still marked the heading where the controversy lies, and have sent you a copy --- so that you might know that the view you said no one had ever held was in fact held by Sex. Aelius, M'. Manilius, and M. Brutus. For all that, I side with Scaevola and Testa.
