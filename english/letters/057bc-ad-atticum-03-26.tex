\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} A letter has been brought to me from my brother Quintus, with the senatus consultum which has been passed about me. My intention is to wait for the legislation, and, if it is opposed, I shall use the authority of the Senate, and shall lack life rather than my country. Hurry, I beg of you, to come to me.
