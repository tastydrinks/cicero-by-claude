\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO MEMMIO S.}

\ciceroSection{1} A. Fufius, one of my closest friends, a man most devoted and most attentive to me, learned and altogether worthy of your friendship, I should like you to treat in the way you assured me in person you would. It will be as welcome to me as anything that could possibly be more welcome; and in him besides --- given his perfect dutifulness and devoted attention --- you will have bound a man to you for life.
