\ciceroLetterOpener{TVLLIVS TIRONI SVO S.}

\ciceroSection{1} I am waiting for your letters on a number of matters --- and for you yourself much more. Send our Demetrius back to me, and whatever else of any good you can manage. On the Aufidius account I have no exhortation for you; I know you have it in hand --- only finish it. And if that is what is keeping you, I accept the cause; if it is not what is holding you, fly here. I am waiting hard for your letter. Farewell.
