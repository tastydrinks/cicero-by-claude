\ciceroLetterOpener{M. CICERO PROCOS. S. D. C. MARCELLO CONLEGAE.}

\ciceroSection{1} That your Marcellus has been made consul, and that you are touched by such joy as you most longed for, gladdens me beyond measure --- both for his own sake, and because I judge you most worthy of every favourable turn, you whose singular goodwill toward me, in my hardship as in my honour, I have seen plainly, and whose whole house I have come to know as the keenest, the most ardent friend of my safety and my standing. You will therefore oblige me by congratulating in my words your wife Iunia, that excellent and admirable woman. From you, I ask what you have always given: that you cherish me in absence, and stand in my defence.
