\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO SERVIO S.}

Hagesaretus of Larisa, distinguished by great benefits at my hand during my consulship, has shown himself mindful and grateful, and has paid me afterwards the most assiduous attentions. Him I commend to you most earnestly: as my host and intimate, a man grateful, a good man, the leading citizen of his town, and most worthy of attachment to you. You will do me a most welcome service if you take care that he understand this recommendation of mine to have carried great weight with you.
