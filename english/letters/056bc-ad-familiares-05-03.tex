\ciceroLetterOpener{Q. METELLVS NEPOS S. D. M. CICERONI}

\ciceroSection{1} The insults of that most insufferable of men, with which he loads me in repeated contiones, are softened by your good offices to me; and being trifling, as they are, coming from a man of his sort, by me they are looked through; and gladly, with the role exchanged, I count you in the place of a brother to me.

\ciceroSection{2} Of him I do not even wish to remember, even though twice I have saved him against his will. As for my own affairs, that I might not be tedious to your circle by a flood of letters, I have written out to Lollius what I want done about the province's accounts, so that he may inform and remind you all of it. If you can, I should be glad if you would keep up your old goodwill towards me.
