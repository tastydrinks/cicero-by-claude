\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Though I have nothing to write to you about, I write all the same, because I seem to be talking with you. Here with me are Nicias and Valerius. Today we were looking out for your morning letter. There will perhaps be a second in the afternoon --- unless the Epirote correspondence is holding you up, which I do not break in on. I have sent over to you the letters to Marcianus and to Montanus. Please add them into the same packet, unless by chance you have already despatched it.
