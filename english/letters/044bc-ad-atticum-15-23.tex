\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} I am racked exceedingly, yet without pain; but a great many considerations occur to me about our journey, on either side. ``How long?'' you will say. As long as the matter is still open; and it will be open until I am at the ship. If Pansa replies to your letter, I shall send you both my own letter and his. I was expecting Silius; for him I have put together a memorandum \textit{[Greek: hypomn\=ema]} in case there is any new development. I have sent a letter to Brutus. About his journey I should be glad to learn from you also, if you know anything.
