\ciceroLetterOpener{BITHYNICUS TO CICERO, his greetings.}

\ciceroSection{1} If I did not have, in my own right, both many and good reasons for friendship with you, I should go back to the beginnings of that friendship in our fathers --- the course I think proper for men who have themselves done nothing in office to carry on a paternal friendship. As it is, I shall be content with the friendship that is ours alone. On the strength of that confidence I ask of you, in my absence, to look after my interests in whatever matters there may be need, if you suppose that any service of yours toward me will not be allowed to die. Farewell.
