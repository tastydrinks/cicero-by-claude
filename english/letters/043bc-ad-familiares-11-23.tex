\ciceroLetterOpener{D. BRVTVS S. D. M. CICERONI}

\ciceroSection{1} We here are doing well, and we shall take pains to do better still. Lepidus appears to be of the right mind toward us. With every fear set aside, we ought now to consult freely for the state. But even if the whole rest of the world were against us, still, with three armies as strong as these, all of them the state's own, you ought to be of great heart --- as you have always been, and as now, with fortune helping, you can rise to be still greater.

\ciceroSection{2} The things I wrote to you in my own hand in my last letter --- people say them only to frighten you. If you take the bit between your teeth, may I die if every one of them put together could endure your attempt to speak. For my part, as I wrote to you before, I shall stay on in Italy until letters reach me from you. 25 May, Eporedia.
