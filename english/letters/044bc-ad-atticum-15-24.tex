\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} The courier I had sent to Brutus came back en route on the seventh day before the Kalends. Servilia told him that Brutus had set out on that very day for \dag his estate\dag. I was truly sorry that my letter was not delivered. Silius had not come to me. I have drawn up the case; I have sent you the document. I should be glad to know on what day to expect you.
