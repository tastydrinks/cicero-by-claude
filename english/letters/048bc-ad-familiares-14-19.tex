\ciceroLetterOpener{TVLLIVS TEREM'IAE SVAE S. D.}

\ciceroSection{1} In the midst of my greatest griefs, our Tullia's health torments me --- about which there is no reason for me to write more to you, since I know for certain it is just as great a care to you. That you wish me to come nearer to you, I see I must do, and would have done before now, except that many things have stood in the way, things which are not even now cleared up. But I am awaiting a letter from Pomponius; please see that it is brought to me as soon as possible. Take pains to keep well.
