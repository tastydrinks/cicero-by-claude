\ciceroLetterOpener{Cicero to Atticus, greetings}

\ciceroSection{1} Approve, please, of my consistency. To watch the games at Antium does not please me; it would be a sub-solecism \textit{[Greek: hyposoloikon]}, when I wish to avoid the suspicion of every kind of luxury, suddenly to show myself \textit{[Greek: anaphainesthai]} as travelling not only luxuriously but ineptly too. So up to the Nones of May I shall await you at the Formian villa. Now make me know on what day we shall see you. From the Forum of Appius, the fourth hour. I had given another a little before from Tres Tabernae.
