\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} As your coming had relieved me, so your departure has cast me down. So when you can --- that is, when you have given your attention to Sextus' auction --- come see me again. Even a single day will be useful to me, not to say welcome. I would come to Rome myself, so that we might be together, if I had taken sufficient counsel on a certain matter.
