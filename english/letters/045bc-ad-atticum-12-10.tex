\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Bad news, by Hercules, about Athamas. Your own grief is human, certainly, but to be kept within bounds. There are many roads of consolation, but the straightest is this: that reason should win the point that time would win in the end. As for Alexis, let us take care of him --- the very image of Tiro --- whom I have sent back to Rome ill; and if the hill carries any \textit{[Greek: epid\=emion]} --- any local infection --- let us shift him to me along with \dag Tisamenus\dag. The whole upper part of the house is empty, as you know. I think this matters greatly to the case.
