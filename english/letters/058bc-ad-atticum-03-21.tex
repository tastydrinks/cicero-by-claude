\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} It was thirty days exactly when I gave this letter, throughout which I had received nothing from you. My intention was, as I have written to you before, to go into Epirus and there to wait above all for whatever might happen. I beg you that, if there is anything you can see clearly --- whichever way it falls --- write me as plainly as you can; and that, in my name, as you write, you give whatever letters you think necessary. Sent the fifth day before the Kalends of November.
