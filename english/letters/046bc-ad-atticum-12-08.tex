\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} About young Cicero --- the plan strikes many people as good. His companion is a suitable one. But about the first installment let us look first. The day is upon us, and the man is in a hurry. Write me, please, what Celer reports Caesar to have done with the candidates --- whether he himself has his thoughts on the Fennel-field of Spain or on the Campus Martius. And I should very much like to know whether it is in any way necessary that I be at Rome for the elections. For I must keep my word to Pilia, and certainly to Attica.
