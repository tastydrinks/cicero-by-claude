\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ACILIO PROCONSVLI S.}

\ciceroSection{1} With the Titurnian family I have a long-standing tie of connection; of that line there survives Marcus Titurnius Rufus, who must be looked after by me with every solicitude and good office. It lies in your hands, then, to see that he reckons he has in me a sufficient protection. I therefore commend him to you in the strongest terms and ask of you that you bring it about that he understands this recommendation of mine to have been of great help to him. That will be deeply welcome to me.
