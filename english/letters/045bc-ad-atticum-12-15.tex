\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} Since the standing excuse does not please him, you will see to it that I am excused before Appuleius day by day. In this solitude I have the conversation of no one, and when in the morning I have hidden myself in a wood, thick and rough, I do not come out of it before evening. Next to you, nothing is more my friend than solitude. In it, all my talk is with books. Even so, weeping breaks in upon it; I fight it off as long as I can, but so far we are no match for each other. To Brutus, as you advise, I shall write back. You will have that letter tomorrow. When there is someone to give it to, you will give it.
