\ciceroLetterOpener{TVLLIVS TIRONI SVO S.}

\ciceroSection{1} I cannot bring myself to write, nor do I want to write, in what state of mind I am. I write only this: that it will be the greatest pleasure both to you and to me if I see you in good strength as soon as possible. On the third day after leaving you we reached Alyzia (the place is a hundred and twenty stadia this side of Leucas). At Leucas I expected to receive either you yourself or a letter from you through Marion. As much as you love me, so much take care to be well --- or as much as you know you are loved by me. The Nones of November, Alyzia.
