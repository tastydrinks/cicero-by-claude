\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO SERVIO S.}

I am on close terms with Asclapo, a physician of Patrae. His company has been agreeable to me, and so too has his skill, of which I have had experience in the illnesses of my own people; and in that he has satisfied me by his knowledge no less than by his loyalty and good will. Him, therefore, I recommend to you, and I ask you to take care that he understand I have written carefully on his behalf and that my recommendation has been of great service to him. That will be deeply welcome to me.
