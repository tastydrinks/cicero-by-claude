\ciceroLetterOpener{TVLLIVS S. D. TERENTIAE SVAE.}

As to what I wrote to you in my last letter about sending the notice of divorce --- what that fellow's power is at the moment, and what the agitation of the crowd, I have no idea. If he is to be feared when angry, hold off; even so, perhaps the move will come from him. You will judge the whole as it stands, and what in these most wretched circumstances you shall reckon the least wretched, that you will do. Farewell. The sixth day before the Ides of Quintilis.
