\ciceroLetterOpener{CICERO ATTICO salutem}

\ciceroSection{1} I reached Astura on the evening of the eighth day before the Kalends. To get out of the heat, I had rested three hours at Lanuvium. Please --- if it will not be a great trouble --- see to it that I do not have to come up there before the Nones (you can manage that through Egnatius Maximus), and above all settle the business with Publilius in my absence. Whatever the talk about that may be, you will write. The People take an interest in it, of course! By Hercules, I do not think so; the story was sung threadbare long ago. But I wanted to fill out the page. What more is there? I myself shall be there, unless you put me off. I have already written to you about the gardens.
